Think Bright ! Recycle Right !

This website is dedicated to raising public awareness about the importance of proper waste management and the negative effects of improper waste disposal on both the environment and human health.

This can be implemented by barangays in focusing on proper waste disposal, recycling, and active participation in community clean-up efforts to significantly reduce litter and prevent flooding. By organizing regular clean-up events and promoting responsible waste disposal, we can help maintain a cleaner and safer environment. Implementing waste management can be achieved through education campaigns, providing accessible waste disposal bins, and encouraging recycling programs at the community level
